# HUAWEI-NUS Innovation Challenge 2023

https://www.sg-innovationchallenge.org/

To create the optimal stock timing model that finds the best trading opportunity 
while providing the lowest overall trading cost of the stock

A simple time average strategy. <br>
Break the target volume into equal parts and execute the order equally across a day. <br>
The buying method and selling method are almost the same here, except the number of parts od_nCount. <br>
You can design different strategies for buying and selling, and try to make the final earning rate higher.
